零空间历史
LAB ZERO story
2013 ~ TechSpace
Shang Song (尚松) opened the place as one of the first makerspaces in Shenzhen. It gathered members from different countries.

TechSpace was focused on providing startups access to factories, organizing supply chain. Because makerspace was not main focus in May 2015 it was closed.
2015 ~ Litchee Lab
After Techspace was closed, Lit Liao (廖丽婷) and other members decided to start Litchee Lab in the same place. Some equipment also was moved from TechSpace.

The unique in this new space was using 1/2 space for makerspace and other half for kids education business which was also owned by Lit. In August 2017 Lit decided to move her kids education business to the new place.

Members faced with the new problem, there is no money to run full space themselves.
2017 ~ LAB ZERO
After Lit's company was moved out, current members decided to rename the makerspace to LAB ZERO and start the next chapter of this story with a new vision of making it a world-class makerspace.
